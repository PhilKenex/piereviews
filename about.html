How It Started

piereviews.co.nz was born from the popular Facebook page "Wellington Pie Reviews," which boasts a community of over 11,000 pie enthusiasts. Expanding beyond Wellington, our website aims to celebrate and review the diverse range of pies found across New Zealand. We're on a mission to uncover the best pies in the country, sharing honest and appetizing reviews with both locals and visitors. From classic flavors to gourmet innovations, join us in our quest to find the perfect pie!
